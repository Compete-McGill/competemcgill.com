Break Your Limits.

Compete McGill (a.k.a Competitive Programming at McGill) is devoted to helping students push their limits in Computer Science and Software Engineering. While we do send teams to represent McGill competitively, our main goal is to train any student, regardless of skill level and prior knowledge - whether their purpose is to compete, to get a job or just to have some fun!

Here for everyone.

At Compete McGill, one of our primary goals is to build a community we have events for all skill levels - so come join the family!

Data Structures and Algorithms

We organize lots of events, year-round (including the Summer) to train your Data Structure and Algorithm Skills, both so that you can compete and for your future careers!

Pictured: Our 2018-2019 ACM-ICPC Teams that brought home 1st Place at Preliminaries!

Competitions: ACM-ICPC, Google CodeJam, Facebook HackerCup...

Technical Skills

We also help you train your technical skills. From Software Engineering to Functional Programming to Security, you'll get a lot of practice that you can apply to your personal events as well!

Pictured: Andre and Bera, who brought home 1st place in the CS Games X-Treme Theory Challenge.

Competitions: CS Games, NorthSec & lots more in Montreal!

Interview Workshops and Mock Interviews

Competitive Programming is fun, but you also need to get a job! We organize lots of workshops and mock interview sessions to give you a helping hand in getting that internship / job!

Members Interning and Working At: Amazon, Google, Microsoft & lots more!
